How it works

Three taps to a better answer next time it counts.

Pick a category

Interview, storytelling, persuasion, technical explanation, off-the-cuff. Verba serves up a prompt you haven't seen.

Talk for sixty seconds

Hit record and go. Transcription runs locally — no buffering, no upload, no spinner waiting on the network.

Read the feedback

Get a breakdown of what worked, what didn't, and specific lines to tighten. Save the session and watch your trends.

The privacy part

Your voice never leaves the device.

Speech recognition uses Apple's on-device Speech framework with the cloud fallback deliberately disabled. Only the transcript text — never audio — is sent for feedback, and even that isn't stored on a server. No accounts, no analytics SDKs, no tracking.
